ZeeZee - Because jewels dress you up

The ZeeZee was born from a dream.
I wanted to create jewels that are Wild and Delicate at the same time, that have personalities and are not bound by the boundaries set by a certain style. They themselves are style.
My name is Bekő Fóri Zenkő, I am an actress and jeweler.
Creating keeps me fresh, it motivates me, creating makes me create. I incorporate myself into every piece of creation, whether it a role or a jewel.
I incorporate the world a little into every piece. I from it to be wearable. Every jewel and role then forms me.
That’s why jewelry is not simply a hobby. For me it’s an artform.
